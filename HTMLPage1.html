About Us?

Makerspace at NIT Raipur is set up on the theme of Open Community Technology Development Culture to promote mainly frugal innovations. It is set up to provide working ecosystem to prospective entrepreneurs of NIT Raipur. Once the raw and innovative ideas will start taking shapes, they will be incubated in a proper system of Technology Business Incubator (TBI).

Benefits of Makerspace

It will help to promote the startups. This will help students to understand challenges of being an entrepreneur and provide them with solutions and resources to build their startup/project from scratch.It will be made open to local communities for technology penetration and accelerate culture of innovations. Encourage students for Inter-Disciplinary representation and forming teams and bringing the expertise to a singular platform. Cost efficient manufacturing of lab equipment like 3-d printers, and other DIY projects.
